Zinger Burger

Description

Fry the chicken at 170c for 5-6 minutes or until cooked through. Make sure the internal temperature reaches at least 75c. Spread some mayo onto the base of a toasted bun, and then add shredded lettuce, the crispy chicken, a slice of cheese. Then finally a bit more mayo before topping with the bun lid.
